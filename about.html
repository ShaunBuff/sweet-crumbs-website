Our Story

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a family-owned bakery based in Parkhurst, Johannesburg. We specialize in cupcakes, breads, cookies, and custom cakes made with love and passion.

Our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality baked goods and create unforgettable moments for every customer.
