About Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a family-owned bakery based in Parkhurst, Johannesburg. We specialize in handmade treats crafted with love and fresh ingredients. From cupcakes to special occasion cakes, our passion is making your sweet moments unforgettable.

Our team is dedicated to quality, community, and creating delicious memories one crumb at a time.
